    FAQ

    • Displayed Data

      Data is fetched from the bitcoinaverage API.

      The rate values are latest trading rates (not average!) on following platforms:

      • Bitcoin Central
      • Bitcoin.de
      • Bitonic
      • Bittylicious
      • BTC-e
      • CleverCoin
      • Cryptonit
      • Hitbtc
      • itBit
      • Kraken
      • LocalBitcoins
      • Rock Trading
      • Vircurex

      The list show those latest trade rates (descending).r

    • Automatic Data Reload

      Data is reloaded in 60 seconds interval. So you can simply leave this site on your screen and watch the rates go up and down.

    • Manually Reload Data

      Click on average or on refresh icon (top right corner) manually triggers data reload.
